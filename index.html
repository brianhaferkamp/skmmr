skmmr users:

Last month our data provider changed our free access to paid access. It was quite unexpected so we've been trying to find a good solution to keep the app running. The app still works we just need to pay for the data that comes in.

After looking at other alternatives to get data we haven't found a better solution than the one we were using. And rather than throw ads all over the app to try and fund it we thought we would hand it over to our user community to help keep the app functioning. Our hope is that the app is unique and valuable enough to keep alive. If just 100 users give $5 a month the app can continue functioning normally.

It feels like a lot to ask, but this has always been a project centered around our users. Allowing the users to be part of the app's life seems right. When you become a patron you'll have even more say on new features and making the app better.
